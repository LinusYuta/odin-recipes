Pan Seared Steak

Description

This Pan-Seared Steak has a garlic rosemary-infused butter that makes it taste steakhouse quality. Youâ€™ll be impressed at how easy it is to make the perfect steak; seared and caramelized on the outside, and so juicy inside.

Ingredients

  • New York Steak
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Rosemary
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Use paper towels to pat the steaks dry to get a perfect sear and reduce oil splatter.
  2. Just before cooking steaks, sprinkle both sides liberally with salt and pepper.
  3. Preheat the pan on medium and brush with oil. Using just 1/2 Tbsp oil reduces splatter.
  4. Add steaks and sear each side 3-4 minutes until a brown crust has formed then use tongs to turn steaks on their sides and sear edges (1 min per edge).
  5. Melt in butter with quartered garlic cloves and rosemary sprigs.
  6. Tilt pan to spoon garlic butter over steaks and cook to your desired doneness.
  7. Remove steak and rest 10 minutes before slicing against the grain.
