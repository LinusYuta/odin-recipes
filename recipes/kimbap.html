Kimbap

Description

Kimbap (or gimbap, 김밥) is a Korean seaweed rice roll filled with a variety of delicious fillings. Gim (김) is a dried seaweed, and bap is rice. With various fillings, these rice rolls have an interesting combination of textures and flavors.

Ingredients

  • Dried seaweed
  • Rice
  • Pickled radish
  • Bulgogi Beef
  • Fish Cake
  • Eggs
  • Carrots
  • Spinach
  • Burdock Root

Steps

  1. Cook the rice slightly drier than normal by using a little less water. Then, lightly season it with sesame oil and salt for a nutty and savory note.
  2. Use 1.5 cups of raw rice for 4 rolls. If you cook 2 cups of rice, you can make 5 or 6 rolls.
  3. Lightly season each ingredient as all the ingredients, including the rice, will be seasoned. Picked radish and burdock roots are especially salty.
  4. Roll it tightly by putting firm, even pressure with your hands all over the roll. Start with a small amount of each filling if this is your first time.
